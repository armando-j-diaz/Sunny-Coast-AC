2. What we collect

When you contact us or download a guide, we may collect:

3. Why we collect it

4. Call & text recording / AI-assisted systems

Florida is a two-party consent state. Calls and texts to Sunny Coast AC may be recorded and/or handled by automated or AI-assisted systems for quality and scheduling. By calling or texting us, you consent to this.

Our phone line should also announce recording where required (operations note).

5. SMS terms (TCPA)

If you check the marketing box on our guide form, you agree to receive occasional seasonal tips and offers by text. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

Consent to marketing texts is not required to get service or to download the guide. The guide/service checkbox is separate and also never pre-checked.

6. How we share information

We use service providers (scheduling, messaging, hosting, automation tools) only as needed to run the business. We do not sell lists. We may disclose information if required by law.

7. Data retention & security

We keep contact and job records as long as needed for service, warranty, and legal obligations. We use reasonable safeguards; no method of transmission is 100% secure.

8. Your choices

  • Opt out of marketing texts anytime (STOP)
