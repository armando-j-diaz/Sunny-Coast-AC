Quick answers

Do you charge for the visit?

There is no trip fee for the in-home appointment. We inspect your system, then walk you through clear options.

Do you do repairs?

We are AC installers. If your system is at the end of its life, we help you replace it correctly. If a repair shop is the better option, we will tell you.

Do you offer financing?

Monthly payments are available on qualifying installs. We review real numbers with you at the house.

What brands do you install?

We install major brands sized for your home and Miami humidity, not whatever is cheapest on the truck.

What areas do you serve?

South Florida, including Miami-Dade, Broward, and nearby communities. Share your city when we call and we will confirm coverage.

How fast can you install?

Timing depends on equipment and the job. After the visit, you will have a clear timeline.
