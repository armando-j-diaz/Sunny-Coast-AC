Questions before you book

What actually happens on the visit?

We inspect your home and your current system.

Then we show clear options: what fits, what it costs, and what financing looks like if you want it.

You decide. There is no pressure to sign on the spot.

Do you do repairs?

We are AC installers. If your system is done or close to it, we help you replace it the right way.

If a repair shop is the better move, we will say so. We would rather earn the install later than sell the wrong solution today.

Is the visit free?

Yes. We come out, inspect, and give you options. There is no trip fee for the appointment.

Do you offer financing?

Monthly payments are available on qualifying installs. We show numbers at the house, not buried in fine print.

What brands do you install?

We install major brands sized for your home and Miami humidity.

What areas do you serve?

South Florida, including Miami-Dade, Broward, and nearby communities. Share your city when we call and we will confirm.

How fast can you install?

It depends on equipment and the job. After the visit you will know a real timeline.
