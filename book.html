Questions before you book

What actually happens on the visit?

It's not a sales speech. We inspect your home and your current system.

Then we show you clear options — what fits, what it costs, and what financing looks like if you want it.

You decide. No pressure to sign on the spot.

Do you do repairs?

We're AC installers. If your system is done (or close), we help you replace it the right way.

If a repair shop is the better move, we'll say so — we'd rather earn the install later than sell you the wrong thing today.

Is the visit free?

Yes. We come out, inspect, and give you options. No trip fee for the appointment.

Do you offer financing?

Monthly payments are available on qualifying installs. We'll show numbers at the house — not hide them in fine print.

What brands do you install?

Quality major brands sized for your home and Miami humidity — not whatever's cheapest on the truck.

What areas do you serve?

South Florida — Miami-Dade, Broward, and nearby. Not sure? Put your city in the form notes when we call — we'll say yes or point you right.

How fast can you install?

Depends on equipment and the job. After the visit you'll know a real timeline — not a vague “soon.”
